# Skill: NSW Exam Domain

Use this skill when working on OC, Selective, NAPLAN, or HSC exam content.

## Rules

- Do not invent official exam timings or formats without verification.
- Treat OC and Selective as V1 scope.
- Treat NAPLAN as V2.
- Treat HSC as V3.
- Preserve exam type as explicit metadata on every question.
- Preserve year level as explicit metadata on every question.

## OC Test Overview

The NSW Opportunity Class Placement Test is sat by Year 4 students applying for Year 5 entry.

Components:

- Mathematical Reasoning
- Thinking Skills
- Reading

Notes:

- Multiple choice format.
- No official calculator use.
- Do not invent specific question counts or time limits without authoritative confirmation.

## Selective High School Test Overview

The NSW Selective High School Placement Test is sat by Year 6 students applying for Year 7 entry.

Components:

- Mathematical Reasoning
- Thinking Skills
- Reading
- Writing

Notes:

- Multiple choice for Mathematical Reasoning, Thinking Skills, and Reading.
- Free-text written response for Writing.
- The Writing component is timed separately.
- Do not invent specific question counts or time limits without authoritative confirmation.

## Writing Component

The Selective writing component is a V1 supported feature.

Supported prompt types:

- Narrative
- Persuasive
- Informative
- Imaginative

AI feedback on writing responses is guidance only. The mandatory disclaimer must always accompany any writing feedback displayed to students or parents:

> "Writing feedback is educational guidance and does not represent official Selective School marking."

## Content Requirements

Every published question needs:

- Correct answer
- Full explanation
- Subject
- Topic
- Difficulty
- Exam type (OC or Selective)
- Year level
