# Subscription Model

## 1. Purpose

Subscriptions determine what exams, subjects, and AI features a parent/student can access.

The parent owns the subscription.

## 2. Account Rule

One parent account can manage up to 3 active student accounts in V1.

Future packages may support more students:

- Family Plus
- School license

## 3. Product Types

### All Access

Includes all available subjects, years, and exam types in the product scope.

### Subject Package

Grants access to one or more subjects.

Examples:

- Mathematics
- English
- Thinking Skills
- Science

### Exam Type Package

Grants access to one or more exam types.

Examples:

- OC
- Selective
- NAPLAN
- HSC

## 4. Billing Periods

Supported:

- Monthly
- Annual

Future optional:

- Lifetime
- School contract

## 5. Premium AI

AI features are Premium-only unless trial usage is configured.

Premium features may include:

- Weakness analysis
- AI tutor
- Practice recommendations
- Readiness score
- Advanced parent reports

## 6. Free Tier

Free users may access:

- One complete sample exam per exam type/year package
- Limited history
- Limited reports
- Limited AI trial usage

## 7. Entitlement Checks

Entitlement checks should occur before:

- Starting an exam
- Viewing explanations under paid content
- Accessing premium AI features
- Viewing advanced reports

## 8. Subscription Ownership

The parent account owns:

- Subscription
- Student access
- Billing relationship
- Student data management rights

## 9. Non-Goals for V1

- School bulk billing
- Teacher classroom licensing
- Coupon engine unless required for launch
- Complex enterprise invoicing
